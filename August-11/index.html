About This Panel

AI is now in the operational core — the question is how to use it well

AI has moved past novelty in aesthetic practices and into the operational core — scheduling, follow-up, reminders, and front desk support. Used well, it removes friction and frees staff for the work that needs a human; used carelessly, it makes patients feel processed rather than cared for.

This panel gives aesthetic owners a practical guide to deploying operational AI where it genuinely helps, while protecting the warmth and trust that define a premium patient experience.
