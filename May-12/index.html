About The Session

Hiring is harder than ever — and your team decides your ceiling

Aesthetic practices that struggle to hire and keep great people face a direct revenue and growth ceiling. Open roles stay open longer, training cycles repeat, and the patient experience suffers.

This panel breaks down what's structurally changed in the aesthetic talent market, what top performers are actually looking for in 2026, and how to build a hiring and retention system that protects both your culture and your bottom line.

Ideal For

  • Aesthetic practice owners & medical directors
  • MedSpa founders & operators
  • Practice managers responsible for hiring
  • Multi-location groups scaling clinical teams
  • Anyone losing top candidates to better-positioned competitors
